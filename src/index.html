COVID-19 Update

The safety of our guests and the incredible vendors we’re working with is of the utmost importance. As of now, we are moving ahead with our wedding as scheduled. We’re keeping very close watch on Colorado guidelines and our venue’s guidelines to ensure it is safe for everyone to gather.


Our venue has made improvements to their site to help ensure safety for all, including social distancing markers at high-traffic areas, increased hand sanitizer and hand washing stations, and new ventilation systems. We’re lucky to be on a large area of open land in beautiful Colorado so there will be plenty of outdoor options to gather and doors will be kept open (weather permitting) to ensure natural ventilation as well.


We highly encourage everyone to get vaccinated before attending our wedding. If you do not or can not receive the vaccine, we highly recommend getting tested either directly before travelling or upon your arrival to Colorado. We will have people travelling from all over the country and the more people vaccinated, the safer we all will be. Masks and hand sanitizer will be made available, and we ask that people please be sure to wash and sanitize hands often. If you are not feeling well, please consider staying home.


If there are any major changes that have to be made due to capacity restrictions or updated guidelines, we will reach out to people individually. If you do not feel comfortable travelling or being around a large number of people, please do not feel pressured to attend our wedding. We want you to feel safe in any circumstance. Depending on conditions in September, we may be offering a livestream of the ceremony for those who are unable to attend or feel more comfortable attending virtually.

FAQ

Q: Are Uber/Lyfts available?

A: Uber and Lyft have limited service in our venue's area. To ensure reliable transportation, we suggest pre-booking your rides prior to attending the event.


Q: What is the dress code?

A: Cocktail attire.


Q: What is the weather like in Colorado in September?

A: Great question. No one really knows! Colorado has very unpredictable weather, but September generally will be in the mid-70s to low-80s. Evenings can get much cooler though, dipping down into the 40s and 50s. There will be a cozy outdoor fireplace at the venue to help keep you warm, but we suggest having some layers handy and be sure to check the weather before you arrive!


Q: Will the altitude affect me?

A: The venue is ~7,300 ft above sea level. If you're not used to high altitude (and even if you are!) drink lots of water. You may feel a little out of breath but we still hope to see you on the dance floor!

Our Story

It all started with a swipe...

Kevin and Melissa met on the dating app Tinder in 2015. Melissa maintains that Kevin's Corgi Link was the deciding factor that caused her to swipe right.

Their first date was at Corner Social in Harlem, NYC (Melissa was early, Kevin was late). They immediately found many things to talk about, from their mutual love for the arts (musicals) to their favorite Rom-Coms (The Holiday). And date #2 was set in no time.

It was during Date #2 that Kevin was introduced to Melissa's love for food, and more specifically: Hatch green chile. Dates 3 and beyond all opened up the many things they had in and also illuminated each's own special interests like Mel's love of film scores and Kevin's love of retro video games.

After over 3 years of dating, Melissa moved in with Kevin and Link into a small but cozy apartment in Astoria, where they currently live. Their time there has been filled with lots of trips to Martha's Country Bakery and Mighty Oak Coffee, plus lots of the other great eateries and small businesses in the area.

One weekend, while Melissa was out of town at a Bachelorette party, Kevin secretly flew to Colorado to ask for her family's blessing to propose to Melissa. Everyone enthusiastically said yes and Kevin was headed back to NYC, all in 24 hours.

Once a ring had been procured, Kevin invited Melissa's family and his parents to witness the proposal. He told Melissa they were going to do one of their favorite date-night activities (BBQ & Broadway) and would be taking her to see Tootsie the Musical. That day they rode the train into work together as normal, but after Melissa got off at her stop, Kevin turned around and headed back to Astoria where he got the final touches of the proposal ready! He picked up Melissa after work at Lincoln Center, and they stopped at the fountain in the main plaza, one of Melissa's favorite spots in all of New York. It was there that he got down on one knee and proposed, much to Melissa's surprise! The surprises continued as their families appeared out of nowhere and then Melissa's brother and sister-in-law surprised them for dinner. It was a night to remember!

In the time of their relationship, Kevin and Melissa have travelled to various states and countries, doing everything from scuba diving in the warm waters of Hawaii to snorkeling between two tectonic plates in below-freezing water in Iceland! They love adventuring and exploring new cultures and especially new foods.

They're excited to continue exploring things big and small together, and have most recently been honing their skills at spoiling their new nieces (Masyn and Drew) and nephew (Meander).

Things To Do

Explore Downtown Denver

  • Denver is a vibrant and wonderful city with lots to explore!
  • Couple's Rec: Take a walk down Larimer Square

Go to the Mountains

  • You can't visit Colorado without taking some time to explore the Rocky Mountains!
  • Couple's Rec: Kev and Mel's favorite mountain town is Breckenridge. Stop by Cool River for a coffee and go see Isak the Troll!

Brewery Tour

  • Did you know some of your favorite beers may have come from Colorado?! Colorado has a huge brewery scene, with everywhere from the biggest brewery in the world, Coors, to great small microbrew houses. Cheers!
  • Couple's Rec: New Belgium Brewery

Go hiking

  • There are countless places you can go hiking in Colorado. Estes Park and Rocky Mountain National Park are two great places to get a true taste of Colorado, plus they're just a short day trip from Downtown Denver!
  • Couples Rec: Mohawk Lake, Breckenridge

Catch a sporting event

  • Need a sports fix? Check out some of the pro sports games you can attend!
  • Couple's Rec: An evening baseball game at Coors Field!

Get some culture in

  • Concerts, museums, art galleries...Denver has it all! Enjoy the lively arts scene in the Denver area
  • Couple's Rec: Anything showing at Denver Center for the Performing Arts
