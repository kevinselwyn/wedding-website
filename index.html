Our Story

It all started with a swipe...

Kevin and Melissa met on the dating app Tinder in 2015. Melissa maintains that Kevin's Corgi Link was the deciding factor that caused her to swipe right.

Their first date was at Corner Social in Harlem, NYC (Melissa was early, Kevin was late). They immediately found many things to talk about, from their mutual move for the arts (musicals) to their favorite Rom-Coms (The Holiday). And date #2 was set in no time.

It was during Date #2 that Kevin was introduced to Melissa's love for food, and more specifically: Hatch green chile.

One weekend, while Melissa was out of town at a Bachelorette party, Kevin secretly flew to Colorado to ask for her family's blessing to propose to Melissa. Everyone agreed and Kevin was headed back to NYC, all in 24 hours.

Once a ring had been procured, Kevin invited Melissa's family and his parents to witness the proposal.
